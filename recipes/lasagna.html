Homemade lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor — try this homemade lasagna recipe today!

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Combine pork and ground beef
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  3. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined.
  4. Assemble lasagna
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 more minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
